Welcome to the Game!

How to Play:

Escape : Pause / Unpause

W / Up Arrow: Move Up
A / Left Arrow: Move Left
S / Down Arrow: Move Down
D / Right Arrow: Move Right

Shift: Slow down
Space Bar: Toggle Shoot
